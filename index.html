Mobile Suit Gundam

Barbatos

Gundam Barbatos is one of the iconic mobile suits from the anime Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans. Piloted by Mikazuki Augus, Barbatos is a versatile and powerful Gundam Frame that was originally built during the Calamity War, over 300 years prior to the events of the series. Its design is sleek yet rugged, equipped with a variety of interchangeable weaponry, allowing it to adapt to different combat situations. As the series progresses, Barbatos undergoes several upgrades and transformations, each enhancing its mobility, firepower, and close-combat capabilities, making it a formidable force on the battlefield. Its connection with Mikazuki showcases a deep bond between pilot and machine, emphasizing the human cost of war.
