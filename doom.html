DOOM ETERNAL

2020

Doom Eternal is a fast-paced first-person shooter and the sequel to the critically acclaimed Doom (2016), developed by id Software. Released in 2020, it continues the story of the Doom Slayer as he battles demonic forces from Hell that have invaded Earth. The game is known for its intense, fluid combat, which encourages players to use a combination of weapons, quick movement, and brutal melee executions to survive against hordes of enemies. Doom Eternal also introduces new mechanics, like the "flame belch" for armor collection and a grappling hook for faster traversal. Its stunning graphics, heavy metal soundtrack, and relentless action make it a standout in the genre.
